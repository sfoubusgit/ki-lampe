# Cover images — locked spec

Article covers are **art-only** images: the cover webp contains *no* text. The
category pill, Fraunces title and **KI•LAMPE** wordmark are drawn as a live CSS
overlay by [`components/CoverCard.tsx`](../components/CoverCard.tsx) (used by the
home grid, `/themen/[slug]`, and category pages). The article hero shows the same
art with the H1 as text — so the title is never baked into the image and never
appears twice.

## Generation recipe (locked 2026-06-28)

The images are generated by the HQ studio, not this repo:

> `mohsen_sina_insta_ai_channel/studio/kilampe_covers/` — `recipe.py` (locked
> params + functions), `prompts.json` (per-article subject prompts), `README.md`.

Locked parameters: **Krea 2 Turbo**, **1216×832** (3:2), 8 steps, WEBP q90, house
style `STYLE` (cinematic indigo/violet, subject on the right, dark negative space
on the left, *no text/words/watermark*). Each article supplies a short subject
prompt; `STYLE` is appended.

To (re)generate, see that folder's `README.md`. New covers are written to the
article's frontmatter `image:` path in both `ki-lampe-v2` and `_deploy_kilampe`,
then committed + pushed here.
